RockWorx Acquisition Ontology + SysML v2 Link: Description back to ToC

A BFO/CCO-conformant ontology of the U.S. DoD acquisition lifecycle (DoDI 5000.85) and its 2025 WAS/JFRP reform, with a working SysML v2 integration.
